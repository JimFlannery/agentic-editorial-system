## 5. Understanding decisions

| Decision | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Accept** | The manuscript is accepted for publication as submitted or with minor copyediting only. |
| **Minor Revision** | Small changes are required. The revised manuscript is typically reviewed by the editor only, not re-sent to reviewers. |
| **Major Revision** | Substantial changes are required. The revised manuscript is re-reviewed, usually by the same reviewers. |
| **Reject** | The manuscript is not suitable for this journal in its current form. The decision letter will explain the reasons. |
| **Reject with Transfer** | The manuscript is not suitable for this journal but may be appropriate for a partner journal. A transfer offer is included in the decision letter. |

Decisions at this journal are made by the Editor or Editor-in-Chief. Reviewer recommendations are advisory — the Editor makes the final decision.
