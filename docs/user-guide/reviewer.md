## 1. Receiving a review invitation

When an Assistant Editor invites you to review a manuscript, you receive an email with:
- The manuscript title and abstract
- The journal name and manuscript type
- The requested deadline for submitting your review
- Links to accept or decline

You do not need to log in to accept or decline — the email contains direct action links.

---

## 2. Accepting or declining

**To accept:** Click the accept link in the invitation email. You will be directed to the reviewer portal where you can access the manuscript files.

**To decline:** Click the decline link. You will be asked (optionally) to suggest an alternative reviewer. Your suggestion will be visible to the Assistant Editor.

**Please respond promptly.** If you do not respond within the time specified in the invitation, the system will send a reminder. If the deadline passes without a response, the system automatically records a non-response and the editorial team will seek an alternative reviewer.

If you are unsure whether you have the right expertise or a potential conflict of interest, decline — it is better to decline than to submit a review outside your area.

---

## 3. Accessing the manuscript

After accepting, log in and navigate to your reviewer portal at `/journal/[acronym]/reviewer`.

Your dashboard shows all active assignments with status badges (Awaiting Response, In Progress, Submitted) and due dates. Click an assignment to open the manuscript detail page, where you can:

- Read the manuscript title and abstract
- Download the manuscript file and any supplementary materials
- See the review deadline and your current invitation status

Blinding arrangements (single-blind or double-blind) are per journal policy — check the invitation email or contact the editorial office if you are unsure whether you should know the authors' identities.

---

## 4. Submitting your review

From the manuscript detail page, click **Submit Review** to open the review form.

The form contains:

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Recommendation** | Accept / Minor Revision / Major Revision / Reject |
| **Comments to the author** | Your detailed review — visible to the author after the decision is sent |
| **Confidential comments to the editor** | Notes for editorial use only — not shared with the author |
| **Conflict of interest confirmation** | Required — confirm you have no undisclosed conflict |

Click **Submit Review** when complete. Your assignment status updates to **Submitted** and the editorial team is notified. You will receive a confirmation email. Once submitted, reviews cannot be edited — contact the editorial office if you need to make a correction.

---

## 5. Deadlines and extensions

The review deadline is shown in your invitation email and in the reviewer portal.

If you need more time, contact the editorial office before the deadline — do not simply submit late. The editorial workflow has automated reminders and escalation steps tied to the deadline; a late review without communication can trigger unnecessary escalation to the Editor-in-Chief.

If you realise mid-review that you cannot complete it, contact the editorial office as soon as possible so they can invite a replacement reviewer with enough time.

---

## 6. Confidentiality

All manuscript content you receive as part of a review invitation is confidential. Do not share, cite, or discuss it until the manuscript is published.

Reviews are confidential unless the journal operates an open review policy. Check the journal's editorial policy for details.
